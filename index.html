About Us

Without You, We’re Nothing

Seven Sea Voyage is a Post-Hardcore/Metalcore band from Denver, Colorado. The band enjoys making songs that happen naturally based on each of its unique musician’s influences. The band is heavily influenced with early 2000’s era music and obsessed with creating songs that fuse the sounds of old with the new. This helps SSV stick out amongst other bands that are popular today. SSV has recently emerged out of a long hiatus consisting of multiple lineup changes. In late 2017, SSV has successfully accomplished the challenging hurdle and has started performing again in the Denver metro area. In less than 6 months out of hiatus, SSV has performed with some of Denver’s heavy hitters including The Anchor, Mosaic, and Letter Nine. SSV has also released 2 music videos reaching over 3k views on YouTube before the release of their new EP Sirens on June 23rd, 2018. SSV continues to improve their sound and live performances with each show they perform. As they continue to play shows, their fan base increases daily on social media and the local Music Scene. SSV’s goal is to inspire their friends to find relief through the music they perform. They believe that through music, it allows them to find peace and solace during tough times. SSV continues to sail forward in spreading their message through their passion. Join us in our voyage as we sail off to find purpose.
